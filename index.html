What is the residual income calculator?
The residual income calculator is a tool that measures a company's residual income.
How to calculate residual income?
To calculate residual income you should use the following formula:
residual income = net income - (equity capital * (cost of equity / 100))
Why use residual income calculator?
The residual income calculator is used in order to value a company.
